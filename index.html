Welcome to Hotel Ranbirs

Luxury Redefined in Gomti Nagar

Hotel Ranbirs, located in the heart of Gomti Nagar, is a spectacular entry point for discreet luxury, celebration & lifetime memorable events. Our 38 elegantly designed rooms deliver an exceptional level of comfort and sophistication.

Strategically located just thirty minutes from Lucknow airport, Hotel Ranbirs occupies a premier position within Lucknow - the "City of Nawabs". We are surrounded by thriving IT and commercial hubs including Tata Consultancy Services, Sahara Hospital and Waves Mall.

Each of our rooms is fully air-conditioned with unlimited WiFi access and equipped with everything you need for a comfortable stay. Our contemporary design with traditional Indian influences creates a unique atmosphere that will leave you with lasting memories.
